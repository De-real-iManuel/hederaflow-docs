## What is Hedera Flow?

Hedera Flow is a **decentralized utility bill verification and payment platform** built on Hedera Hashgraph. It combines AI-powered OCR, fraud detection, and blockchain transparency to revolutionize how consumers verify and pay electricity bills in emerging markets.

## The Problem

In developing regions (Nigeria, India, Brazil), utility billing fraud costs consumers **$2.3B annually**:
- **Estimated bills** without meter readings lead to overcharging
- Manual verification is time-consuming and error-prone
- No transparent audit trail for disputes
- Payment reconciliation takes weeks

## Our Solution

Hedera Flow provides:
- **AI-Powered Verification**: OCR + fraud detection for instant meter reading validation
- **Blockchain Transparency**: Immutable audit logs on Hedera Consensus Service (HCS)
- **Instant Payments**: HBAR-based payments with real-time settlement
- **Regional Compliance**: Multi-currency support (EUR, USD, NGN, INR, BRL) with localized tariffs
